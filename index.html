Instructions

  • Players have the option to edit their names by clicking the "edit names" button
  • Click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice
  • Each player gets to play twice then pass it over
  • Click "Switch" to move to the next player
  • The sum of the dice will be the points players get
  • Player with the highest points wins
  • Click "Restart" to restart the game
